Proper Running Form

Why is Form so Important?

Form is what often takes a runner from just being good to being great. When you reach the top of the sport every advantage that you can get is needed. If you can be just 1% more efficient that makes a huge difference whether you are looking to drop .01 of your 100m time or running a marathon. Form is also one of the most difficult skills to perfect. Trying to change and perfect form is one the hardest things to do in running and is a never ending process.

What is Good Form?

The main purpose of running is to go forward faster than anyone. Form is all about using the energy that you create the most effecient way possible. Any movement that is moving side to side is wasting energy. As you can see in the picture of Bolt to the right, the angles that you land and take off at are extermely important when it comes to energy return. Your overall posture and where your foot lands and the position of your foot are all key parts to running form.

How to Improve Form

Improving your running is form a long and arduous process that takes place over many years. The first step is focusing on form while you are training. That way when it comes time to race it comes naturally to you instead of of thinking about it. Doing form drills like different types of skips and other exercises will help reinforce the important basics to good running form. Stength training can also be very effective because keeping good form is difficult while racing and requires good core strength to keep you upright.
